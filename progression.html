Training Progression

A structured path from first skills to race confidence, with clear milestones at each stage.

1

Foundations

Water comfort, breathing rhythm, and balanced body position.

2

Stroke Dev

Freestyle/backstroke patterns and coordinated stroke timing.

3

Endurance

Longer repeats, pace awareness, and consistent technique under effort.

4

Race Prep

Starts, turns, breakouts, and confidence for competition pathways.

Stage Benchmarks Coach Focus
Foundations Comfortable submersion, body line control, stable kick pattern. Safety habits, confidence, repeatable form.
Stroke Dev Rhythmic breathing and cleaner freestyle/backstroke mechanics. Timing, streamline, efficient propulsion.
Endurance Sustained technique over sets with reduced breakdown. Pacing, stamina, form consistency.
Race Prep Reliable starts/turns and confident race simulations. Execution, composure, and performance readiness.
