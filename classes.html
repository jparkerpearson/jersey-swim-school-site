Program Tracks

One idea per class: safety, skill, consistency, then performance.

🛟 Foundations

Turtle Tots and Minnows classes focus on comfort, breathing, and body control.

🏊 Technique

Jellyfish and Sharks build stroke mechanics, timing, and efficient movement.

🏁 Competitive Prep

Sea Otters and Swordfish introduce pace work, turns, and race readiness.

Class Approx Age Prerequisite Description
Turtle Tots Kindergarten & Under No prerequisite Parent-assisted class covering comfort in water, bubble blowing, floating, and safe movement.
Minnows 1-2 Kindergarten & Under Intro level Kicking, rotary breathing, foundational arm stroke patterns, and balanced body position.
Jellyfish 1-3 9 & Under Basic comfort and propulsion Freestyle/backstroke development, breathing control, and intro endurance sets.
Sharks 1-3 9 & Under Consistent freestyle/backstroke Stroke refinement, stronger kick integration, and confidence over longer repeats.
Sea Otters 1-3 10 & Over Intermediate stroke control Technique consolidation with endurance progression and turn/breakout emphasis.
Swordfish 10 & Over Advanced readiness Race prep, pace work, starts/turns, and transition toward competitive team expectations.
Adult Lessons 18+ All levels welcome Beginner to advanced adult tracks for safety, confidence, and stroke efficiency.
