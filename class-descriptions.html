Class Descriptions

Every class focuses on water safety, confidence, strong technique, and measurable progress.

Group Lesson Levels

Class Approx Age Prerequisite Description
Turtle Tots Kindergarten & Under No prerequisite Parent and child experience an active 30-minute, instructor-guided class.
Focus areas include comfort on and under water, bubble blowing, floating, body position, balance, and propulsive kicks.
Minnows 1 Kindergarten & Under No prerequisite For students new to water who have not fully submerged or cannot kick on their back 50 feet using a propulsive kick.
Includes kicking front/back, sculling, rotary breathing, and arm strokes.
Minnows 2 Kindergarten & Under Comfortable in water, face in, and kicking on back Students pass Minnows 1 skills and continue with stronger flutter kicks (front/back), bilateral breathing, breaststroke kicking, and drills for balanced body position.
Minnows 3 Kindergarten & Under Freestyle and backstroke For swimmers already performing freestyle with propulsive kicks and bilateral breathing.
Adds elementary backstroke, somersaults, and drills for breaststroke and butterfly.
Jellyfish 1 9 & Under No prerequisite For students new to water who have not fully submerged or cannot kick on their back 50 feet using a propulsive kick.
Includes kicking front/back, sculling, rotary breathing, and arm strokes.
Jellyfish 2 9 & Under Comfortable kicking/sculling on back and rotary breathing on front Students pass Jellyfish 1 skills.
Focus on strong propulsive flutter kicks, bilateral breathing, breaststroke kicking, and drills for balanced body position.
Jellyfish 3 9 & Under Freestyle and backstroke For swimmers already performing freestyle with propulsive kicks and bilateral breathing.
Adds elementary backstroke, somersaults, and drills for breaststroke and butterfly.
Sharks 9 & Under Strong/proper freestyle and backstroke, legal breaststroke kick and dolphin kick for 200 feet each without stopping Activities include coordinating breaststroke and butterfly, flip turns, open turns, breakouts, and drills for strength and endurance.
Sea Otters 1 10 & Over No prerequisite Students may be new to water or still learning breath control, balance, and propulsive kicks.
Includes kicking front/back, sculling, bilateral breathing, arm strokes, elementary backstroke, and drills for balanced body position.
Sea Otters 2 10 & Over Freestyle and backstroke For swimmers already doing freestyle with propulsive kicks and bilateral breathing.
Adds breaststroke kicking, dolphin kicking, somersaults, and refinement of backstroke and freestyle.
Swordfish 10 & Over Strong/proper freestyle and backstroke, legal breaststroke kick and dolphin kick for 200 feet each without stopping Activities include coordinating breaststroke and butterfly, flip turns, open turns, breakouts, and drills for strength and endurance.
Adult Levels Adults Any level welcome Activities focus on body position, breathing, and kicking, which lead into stroke building and refinement.

Private & Semi-Private Lessons

  • Private Lessons: One-on-one coaching with a customized plan built around the swimmer's goals.
  • Semi-Private Lessons: Two swimmers with similar level/goals trained together with focused instruction.
