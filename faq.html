Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about classes, enrollment, scheduling, makeups, and billing.

Programs & Placement

What ages do you teach?

From 6 months through adults, with level-based tracks.

How do I pick the right level?

Start with a free level assessment and coach recommendation.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes, one free trial class is available for new swimmers.

Are private lessons available?

Yes, private and semi-private options are offered based on schedule availability.

How many children are in each class?

Group classes have no more than 6 students. Jr. Clinics/Sea Stars have 7–10 students.

How long are the swim lessons?

Group lessons are 30 minutes. Clinics (Jr. Clinic/Sea Stars) are 45 minutes.

Scheduling & Enrollment

How long are lessons?

Most group lessons are 30 minutes; select clinic tracks are longer.

Can I join mid-session?

Yes, rolling enrollment is available with prorated billing when applicable.

Can we switch levels or times?

Yes, transfers can be requested and are confirmed based on availability and level fit.

What if the facility closes?

Affected lessons are credited or made up according to center policy.

On Deck & Billing

Can parents watch lessons?

Yes, poolside and lobby viewing is available.

What should swimmers bring?

Swimsuit, towel, goggles, and any personal comfort gear approved by staff.

How is billing handled?

Billing is handled through your account portal with support from the front desk.

How do I get help quickly?

Contact the front desk by phone or email for immediate assistance.
