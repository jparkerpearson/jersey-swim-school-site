JAC Swim School: Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about programs, placement, scheduling, enrollment, and billing.

Programs & Placement

Question Answer
What age groups do you offer swim lessons for?We offer swim lessons for children starting at 6 months up to 18+ (Adults).
What are the different swim levels or groups?Class Description List.
How do I know which level my child should be enrolled in?We offer a free level assessment or you can refer to level descriptions on the registration page. Staff will help place your child.
How long does it usually take to level up?Progress varies for each individual. There is no set timeframe for advancing levels.
Do you offer trial classes?Yes, one free trial class is available. Contact Phone (973) 487-6954 or Email info@jerseyaquaticcenter.com for more info.
Do you offer lessons for children with special needs?Yes, students with special needs are encouraged to join group lessons with accommodations available, or opt for private lessons.
Is it required for parents to get in the water with their child?Parental participation is required only for Turtle Tots.
What should my child bring to swim class?Appropriate one piece suits & goggles. Other recommendations include: cap, rubber pants, towel, nose plugs/earplugs unless medically necessary.
Are parents allowed on the pool deck?Yes, seating is available poolside and in the lobby.
What safety measures are in place?Lifeguards are present at all times, and strict cleanliness and safety protocols are followed.
Do you offer private or semi-private lessons?Yes, see pricing information below under billing information.

Scheduling & Structure

Question Answer
How long are the swim lessons?Group lessons are 30 minutes. Clinics (Jr. Clinic/Sea Stars) are 45 minutes. Private lessons are typically 30 minutes and can be extended upon request.
How many children are in each class?Group classes have no more than 6 students. Jr. Clinics/Sea Stars have 7–10 students.
Can I register after the session has started?Yes, we offer rolling enrollment and prorated fees.
What is the process to transfer classes?You can request a transfer (time or level) by contacting the front desk or submitting a request through the customer portal. Level changes require instructor recommendation and all transfers are subject to availability.
What happens if the aquatic center is closed (weather, maintenance, or holidays)?If we close for any reason, you will not be billed for affected lessons and will receive a make-up class or account credit. We are closed on some holidays, and any missed classes due to these closures will be prorated in your billing. Please contact us for any specific questions.

Registration & Enrollment

Question Answer
How do I register for swim lessons?You can register online through our website, call us directly, or visit the front desk during business hours. We recommend enrolling online to streamline the process and familiarize yourself with the system. If you have any issues, our team is happy to assist.
What is your policy on dropping or withdrawing from classes?We require a 30 days’ notice for withdrawal.
Can I pause my enrollment?No, we do not allow temporary pauses. Any request to pause must follow the standard withdrawal policy, which requires 30 days’ notice.
Am I allowed to register my child for more than 1 class a week?Yes. We recommend multiple classes per week to accelerate progress. A 25% multi-class discount is available for families enrolled in more than one class per week.

Billing

Question Answer
How much are classes?
  • Group lessons are $40/class
  • Jr. Clinic/Sea Stars is $50/class
  • Private lessons are $100 per 30 minutes
  • Semi-private lessons are $75 per child
  • Sea Stars pricing varies, please contact us for details
How am I billed?
  • Billing is monthly and based on the number of scheduled classes (example: $40 Monday class would cost $160 in a 4-week month or $200 in a 5-week month).
  • Mid-month enrollments are prorated.
  • Billing occurs within the first 2 days of each month.
Can I pay with cash?Cash is not accepted for most swim school enrollments. Accepted payment methods include credit/debit card, bank account, or online payments via the customer portal. Cash is only accepted for Sea Stars and Family Swim programs.
Is there a discount for enrolling multiple children?Yes. Sibling discount: $2.50 off per class for each additional child. Multi-class discount: 25% off when a student is enrolled in more than one class per week.
What is your refund policy?Refunds are not issued under any circumstances. A credit may be applied to your account at management’s discretion based on the situation. Credits can only be used for in-facility purchases.
What is your make-up lesson policy?Two make-up lessons per month are allowed with at least 24 hours’ advance notice. The class will be marked as an excused absence, and after it ends, a make-up token (valid for 30 days) will be added to your account. Make-ups must be scheduled after the missed class and are subject to availability. Missed classes without notice are not eligible for make-ups or compensation, except in emergencies with a doctor’s note.
